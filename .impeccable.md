## Design Context

### Users
Payby serves professional creators, small studios, and community operators who need to publish premium media assets from a wallet-connected workspace. They upload and manage files, validate where the content is stored, and share access paths with buyers or members.

### Brand Personality
Refined, fast, trustworthy. Payby should feel like a serious creator finance and media operations product, not a toy upload demo.

### Aesthetic Direction
The interface uses a restrained editorial-tech style with strong contrast, precise spacing, and operational density. The landing page is separate from the app workspace, and the product supports both light and dark modes.

### Design Principles
- Make storage status and network selection visible at all times.
- Treat upload as a professional publishing workflow, with clear readiness, file review, expiration, and error states.
- Keep the landing page persuasive and focused while keeping the app workspace utilitarian.
- Do not hide integration details; Shelby RPC, Aptos fullnode, and indexer endpoints should be inspectable.
- Design for production posture even while Shelby networks are early-access/prototype environments.
